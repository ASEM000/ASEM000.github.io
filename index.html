Research interests

My research spans several different areas involving fluid mechanics, energy, and deep learning. Currently, I experimentally study the effects of stratified environments that mimicks ocean conditions on the behavior of jets and plumes pollutants. I employ deep learning to improve data quality in the experiments phase and understand the underlying physics in the analysis phase. Moreover, I am interested in the coupling of traditional numerical methods and deep learning methods for faster and better partial differential equations solutions.
